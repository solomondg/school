"n'a pas"

    Mais si = Yes I do (in response to negative)

"ne... aucun(e)" = none/not any
"ne... jamais" = never (not ever)
"ne... nulle part" = nowhere (not anywhere)
"ne... personne" = no one (not anyone)
"ne... que" = only
"ne... rien" = nothing (not anything)
